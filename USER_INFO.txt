Chess Helper is a simple chess assistant app.

# What this app does
Chess Helper shows the best move suggestion for the current position using the Stockfish chess engine.
The app does not play automatically - it helps you choose a stronger move.

# What it is for
- Quickly get a suggestion for the best move right now.
- Analyze positions during a game or training session.
- Review your games and validate your ideas more easily.

# How to use (simple and fast)
1. Launch the app.
2. Select the side you play: use the "Play as Black" toggle if needed.
3. Recreate real game moves on the board (both yours and your opponent's) by moving pieces.
4. When it is your turn, a hint will appear automatically on the right.
5. The board also shows an arrow for the recommended move.
6. Make the move on your real board/site, then enter it in the app and continue.

# Buttons
- "New Game" - start from the initial position.
- "Undo Move" - revert the last move.

# Short usage scenario
You play on one device, and on another device you open Chess Helper, repeat the moves, and get a suggestion for your next move.
